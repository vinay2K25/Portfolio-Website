About Me

I am a Computer Science undergraduate at IIIT Hyderabad with a strong interest in software engineering, problem solving and building real-world applications. My programming journey began in 2023 with a small Python game project, which sparked a curiosity for creating things through code. Since then, I have explored C, C++, Python, web-development, data structures, and algorithms through course-work and personal projects. I enjoy learning by building and continuously challenging myself through new technologies and projects. Outside of programming, I enjoy table-tennis, tennis and football.
